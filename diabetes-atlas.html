Diabetes in America — At a Glance
Diabetes Belt
15.4%
Average diabetes prevalence in the 15-state Diabetes Belt (AL, AR, FL, GA, KY, LA, MS, NC, OH, SC, TN, TX, VA, WA, WV) — vs 10.2% in non-Belt states.
Prevention Potential
58%
Of Type 2 diabetes cases are preventable through lifestyle changes — diet, exercise, and modest weight loss can delay or prevent onset in high-risk individuals.
Rural vs Urban Gap
+17%
Rural residents have 17% higher diabetes prevalence than urban counterparts, driven by reduced healthcare access, lower physical activity opportunities, and food deserts.
Economic Burden
2.6×
Americans with diabetes have medical costs 2.6× higher than those without. Diabetes accounts for 1 in every 4 healthcare dollars spent on Americans aged 65+.
